01 / About

A stage for
new voices.

SFFB is a student-run festival highlighting original Bahraini cinema. We program shorts, features, and experimental work from filmmakers still finding their voice.

Every year we bring the community together for screenings, filmmaker Q&As, and workshops that make cinema feel possible.
